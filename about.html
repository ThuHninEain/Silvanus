Our mission

Our mission is to encourage all the people to plant trees and other types of plants we provided information about. We want you to tell a friend or family member to protect our human civilization, habitat for biodiversity and restore forests through sharing the knowledge guide that makes it simple for anyone to help the environment by donating to the websites we linked to.

Our vision

We see a future where this project plays a role in donation websites gaining an immense amount of increase in their donations. We hope to get at least one person to start challenging climate change by creating this website. The complete extinction of climate change has a very small possibility but we can increase it by planting more trees.

How did we come up with this?

We were influenced by a lot of websites created to help everyone with anything related to nature including our planet. One morning, we were discussing what we all love and want the earth to be improved by developing a website. Later, we realised that climate change is a big issue all around the world, but instead of only spreading awareness, we decided to attempt another course of action which is the idea of presenting about trees.
