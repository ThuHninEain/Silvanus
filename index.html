Awareness

What is climate change? What is deforestation? How does deforestation work? What is the cause of it? Are we running out of trees? What is the situation around the world?

We want you to be aware of the fact that the world is going through a major existential threat, and how we respond to it will determine what our future looks like and that is called climate change.

Climate change and deforestation are two of the most pressing environmental issues facing the world today. As temperatures continue to rise, extreme weather events like hurricanes, droughts, and wildfires become more frequent and severe. Meanwhile, deforestation and forest degradation are causing significant biodiversity loss and contributing to the release of greenhouse gases.

What can we do?

We will be explicitly explaining the course of action you can take to help rebuild forests and restore land so the benefit of healthy tropical forest can be felt worldwide. If it only takes one person to make a change, imagine how much progress we could make for this earth if there were thousand more people? If you're unaware of the situation all over the world, please go to the 'Information Page' then check the contents, and read the information you need to explore. There are two paths you can take if you want to help the planet: donating & planting. By donating, you can help organisations plant trees and help the planet if you struggle with quality time. On the contrary, by planting, it could exercise you and lead you to doing more outdoor activities. By then, you will be a lot healthier either mentally or physically as well as making your environment a better place.

These very affordable donation websites and companies are trying their best to make it simple for anyone to help the environment by planting trees. With that we can restore forests, create habitat for biodiversity, and make a positive social impact around the world.

#TeamTrees

In this website, there are millions of trees being planted all over the world, thanks to their partnership with the Arbor Day Foundation. Donating $1 plants 1 tree.

ONETREEPLANTED

One Tree Planted is a non-profit environmental charity on a mission of global reforestation. They plant trees in North America, Latin America, Africa, Asia, Europe and the Pacific. Donating $1 plants one tree for this organization.

TREES FOR THE FUTURE

This website can train more farmers, plant more trees, and sustainably restore more landscapes with the donation money that you can pay one time or monthly.
