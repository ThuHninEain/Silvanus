#TEAMTREES

Interested? Donate to #TEAMTREES

  • What they do
  • Creator
  • Impact

#TEAMTREES is a well known website due to its affordable donations which is a dollar for a tree. They started this website on 24th May, 2019 when a fan suggested MrBeast plant 20 million trees in an honour of getting 20 million subscribers.

MrBeast & Mark Rober

#TEAMTREES has succeeded in planting 24,409.201 trees with an immense amount upgrading each day within the past 3 years and over.

ONE TREE PLANTED

Interested? Donate to ONE TREE PLANTED

  • What they do
  • Creator
  • Impact

'One Tree Planted' plant a tree per dollar the user donates. They also provide information about trees so people in the environment can have easier access to tutorials and plant trees which will upgrade the environment.

Matt Hill, MBA & MSC

They have been able to plant over 92,000,000 plants in over 80 countries.

TREES FOR THE FUTURE

Interested? Donate to TREES FOR THE FUTURE

  • What they do
  • Creator
  • Impact

This website allows users to donate to plant trees by different amounts- starting from £9-£90. Additionally, they spread knowledge about the benefits of trees and reasons why we should plant trees. They also train farmers to be more aware of the usage of land which would lead them to grow plants that are fit for the habitat.

Dave & Grace Deppner

'Trees for the Future' has managed to work with farming families for better growth of plants and income for the said families

TREES4TREES

Interested? Donate to TREES4TREES

  • What they do
  • Creator
  • Impact

Similar to #TEAMTREES, users can donate by dollars and a tree will be planted per dollar. Moreover, they advertise the advantages of planting trees.

Sustainable Green Earth Foundation (Yayasan Bumi Hijau Lestari)

3,193,959 trees have been planted with the help of the website 'TREES4TREES'. From the total of trees they've planted, they've captured a total of 481,860 tonnes of carbon dioxide in the atmosphere.
